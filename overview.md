The EM Series is Zongheng Intelligent Control (IOTRouter)’s next-generation modular edge computing gateway series (FlowPLC®), designed for industrial data acquisition, protocol conversion, and PLC/CNC integration applications.

It is built on a modular and expandable architecture that integrates communication, data acquisition, control, and remote operations into a unified industrial edge device.

EM300 is the core model of the EM Series, designed for distributed industrial environments requiring multi-protocol connectivity, flexible I/O expansion, and reliable edge computing capabilities.
